Touhou Project Popularity Poll

This is an unofficial online event where you can vote on the popularity of characters and music that appear in the Touhou Project.

Since the first round of voting was held in 2003, it has been held regularly, approximately once a year. Anyone can participate for free. Voting will be held in three categories: the human spirit category, the music category, and the work category, and the number of votes, ranking, and comments accompanying the vote will be announced.

You can predict the result, create a work that supports your favorite character, and enjoy the voting comments of the same character as yourself.

I hope it will continue to be one of the places where Touhou fans can interact.
